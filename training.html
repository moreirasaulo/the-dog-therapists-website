Puppy class

Teaching social skills and etiquette for puppies under 6 months.

(10 weeks to 5 months old) A great introductory class that teaches you how to communicate with your dog & basic skills like "come when called" & loose-leash walking.

Your puppy will learn to:

  • How to behave nicely to familys and your friends
  • Not to chew objects
  • Playing games and tricks to impress
  • How to behave outside
  • Basic commands like Sit, lie down and come
  • Nice behaviour when they see vets, groomers and family members

Junior Class

Teaching social skills and etiquette for puppies under 12 months

(5 months & older) For dogs with no previous training, we will teach basic manners with impulse control & relationship building exercises. Emphasizing focus, loose-leash walking, "come when called" & "leave it".

Your dog will learn to:

  • How to behave nicely to familys and your friends
  • Not to chew objects
  • Playing games and tricks to impress
  • How to behave outside
  • Basic commands like Sit, lie down and come
  • Nice behaviour when they see vets, groomers and family members

Basic dog class

Teaching social skills and etiquette for dogs over 12 months.

It begins with our basics classes aka our obedience training class, enables the dog owner to gain control over the family dog.

Your dog will learn to:

  • To come even when distracted
  • To sit while guests visit
  • To stay and wait even when distracted
  • To stay calm on new people
  • To walk outside quitely beside you
  • To play and settle down on command

Problem prevention

Providing solutions to problematic behavior.

Sometimes dogs can suddenly exhibit bad behaviors—even dogs who are otherwise on their best behavior. Since dogs have no sense of morality, they don't do something because it's "wrong" or "right." They just do whatever works.

Our cessions will help you if your dog hehaves:

  • Anxiety to seperation
  • Aggressive behaviour towards people
  • Frequent barking
  • Difficult to make new dog friends
  • OCD (Obsessive Compulsive Disorders)

Home visiting sessions

In-home consultations are offered to provide you with the convenience of having a professional dog trainer come to your home. Certain cases are also better dealt with in the dog's own environment. If the weather is bad or you do not have transportation or feel the car ride is too stressful for your dog we can go to you. Sometimes it may also be easier to get the whole family together at home when it comes to choosing a time for your classes. Obedience, behavioural issues as well as puppy manners can all be dealt with in home.
