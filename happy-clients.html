Our happy clients

Click on images to learn how our training helped to improve their lives


Sebastian

Sebastian was a 3 year old healthy and energetic dog, but he had an issue: every day, when his owner was leaving to work, Sebastian used to become over-anxious and started destroying everything he could find in front of him. After only 3 weeks of training, he is now a very calm dog.

Sarah

Sarah is a lovely doggy, but she had a problem: whenever someone was approaching her owner, she used to become ferocious! We had the pleasure of taking care of her and after only 10 days she stopped biting her owner's friends!

Cabrito

Cabrito was not so much responsive to games, and we could train him to seat, roll, play dead and much more during the whole month we had the pleasure to be with him!

Rex

Rex is a Brazilian Mastiff who had problems to be comfortable in the presence of visitors. After only 5 weeks of training, Rex became very docile with everyone, especially the kids!

Aristotle

Our friendly Aristotle here had one single problem: he wasn't able to go to the "toilet", or even control his emotions when his owner was arriving home! In only 15 days this smart doggy learnt how to do it when nature calls!

Simone

This beautiful lady is called Simone. Aside her beauty, she was having hard time to learn the tricks to participate in the contest for the smartest dog. We trained her for 3 months and 2 weeks to make her the gold medal of Montreal's Kennel Club!

Lucky

Lucky is a very friendly and companion dog, but her habit of barking too often and too loud was driving her neighbors crazy! Of course we didn't do any surgery on her (we also don't think that's fair with our friends!) but trained her instead. In just 6 weeks she became obedient and more respectful to everyone!

Casper

This beautiful Siberian Husky named Casper became the most popular dog of his village after our remote training. Moreover, he became the local hero for saving a baby from the flames of a fire! Can you believe that?! And there's more to this thrilling story. The parents of the then baby - now with 4 years old - keep visiting him regularly to play with him and show all their gratitute!

Layla

Layla had a perfect life for a doggy: very much loved by her owners, food, comfort and a lot of space to play... but, for some reason, she used to present severe behavioural problems, sometimes being strongly aggressive to people. After our 5 weeks intensive care, she is now able to walk calmly on the streets without representing a threat to her neighborhood!

Lemming

Lemming is a very curious and friendly dog, but he literally couldn't wait his owners come back from work! After a huge mess done by the frightened doggy in a common day of work, his owners decided to ask for our help... and guess what? Only ater 10 days with our assistance, Lemming was able to relax with his toys and wait patiently for his friends to arrive home!

Laika

Laika doesn't have this name by chance, she's a true explorer! Or at least, she was. Her exploring nature was giving a lot of headache to her owners, since they couldn't walk her peacefully: she always needed to run to other people, dogs, cats, flying leaves... basically everything crossing her sight. After only 16 days she learnt to behave and now she knows perfectly when she's allowed to have fun!

Samuel

Don't judge Samuel by his appearence. Before our visit, he was the angriest dog of his neighborhood. Threatening (and sometimes biting!) anyone who could dare to get closer to his owner, Samuel finally understood that people don't represent danger just because they're not stroking him. And all that in just 5 days!
